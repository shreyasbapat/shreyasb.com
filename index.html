EinsteinPy

An open source Python library for general relativity and gravitational physics. Supports geodesic calculations in Schwarzschild, Kerr, and Kerr–Newman geometries, symbolic tensor algebra, and spacetime visualization. Used by researchers and students worldwide. Started as a research project at IIT Mandi and grew into a community project under OpenAstronomy.
