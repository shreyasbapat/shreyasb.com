01 / About

A bit about me

I'm Shreyas — a software engineer with a deep curiosity for how systems scale and how open source can change the world. I graduated from IIT Mandi with a B.Tech. in Electrical Engineering and a minor in Management.

Currently I'm a Senior Software Engineer at Tekion Corp, working on the FixedOps team building automotive dealership software. I work primarily with Go and distributed systems at scale.

Outside of work I'm passionate about open source. I'm the creator and maintainer of EinsteinPy — a Python library for general relativity and gravitational physics — and I helped build the Computational Astrophysics group at IIT Mandi.

When not at a keyboard I'm usually on a bike ride, chasing mountain trails, or somewhere in the Himalayas.
